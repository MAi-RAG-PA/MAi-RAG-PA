Morning dew on grass,
Sunlight wakes the sleeping world—
New day starts again.

Autumn leaves fall down,
Crimson dancing in the wind,
Winter comes soon now.

Moon shines through the trees,
Silver light upon my face,
Night is calm and deep.

Rain taps at window,
Puddles form on quiet streets,
Clouds roll overhead.

Snow falls softly white,
Blanket covers all the earth,
Silence fills the air.

Wind whispers to pines,
Branches sway in gentle dance,
Nature sings its song.

Waves crash on the shore,
Salt spray kisses rocky cliffs,
Ocean breathes and sighs.

Stars twinkle above,
Night sky wears a diamond crown,
Dreamers close their eyes.

Fire crackles warm,
Golden flames light up my face,
Winter fades away.

Bird sings from branch high,
Morning calls to all the world—
Life begins anew.
